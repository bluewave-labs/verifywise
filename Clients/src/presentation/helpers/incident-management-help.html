Incident Management

The Incident Management module allows users to create, track, and manage AI-related incidents across multiple projects, ensuring compliance with the EU AI Act Article 73. This is a core compliance component for the VerifyWise platform.

Summary Cards

Summary cards at the top display counts of incidents by status:

  • Open: Incidents currently being investigated
  • Investigating: Incidents under active assessment
  • Mitigated: Incidents where corrective actions have been applied
  • Closed: Fully resolved incidents

Adding New Incidents

To add a new incident:

  1. Click the "Add New Incident" button
  2. Fill in the required fields:
    • AI Project: Name of the AI project involved
    • Incident Type: Type of incident (e.g., Malfunction, Misuse, Model Drift)
    • Severity: Level of severity (Minor, Serious, Very Serious)
    • Date Occurred: When the incident happened
    • Date Detected: When the incident was detected
    • Reporter: Person reporting the incident
    • Categories of Harm: Select at least one applicable category (Health, Safety, Rights, Property, Environment)
    • Description: Detailed description of the incident
    • Relationship / Causality: How the AI system caused or contributed to the incident
    • Immediate Mitigations: Actions taken immediately to address the incident
    • Planned Corrective Actions: Actions planned to prevent recurrence
    • Approval: Approval status, approved by, and notes (if applicable)
  3. Click "Save Incident" to store the record

Managing Incidents

For each incident in the table, you can:

  • Edit: Click a row or use the actions menu to update incident details
  • Archive: Remove incidents from the active list (requires confirmation)
  • Filter & Search: Use Severity, Status, or Approval filters and search by project, reporter, or incident ID

Status & Severity Colors

Each status and severity level has a visual badge:

  • Severity: Minor (green), Serious (orange), Very Serious (red)
  • Status: Open (blue), Investigating (yellow), Mitigated (teal), Closed (gray)
  • Approval: Pending (orange), Approved (green), Rejected (red), Not Required (gray)

Compliance & Validation

The module enforces EU AI Act Article 73 requirements. Key validation rules include:

  • Required fields must be completed before saving
  • Date Occurred cannot be in the future
  • At least one Harm Category must be selected
  • All actions are tracked with reporter, approval, and timestamps for auditing
